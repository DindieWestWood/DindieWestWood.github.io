About me

In 2014 I was preparing my Bachelor in Computer Science, at the University of Toulouse, when I discover the field of Human Computer Interaction (HCI). 2 years later I succesfully graduted from the French School of Civil Aviation (ENAC) and got my Master Degree in HCI.

I subsequently started my career, in the User Experience research team of ruwido (an Austrian remote control manufacturor). There, I developed my skills in User Experience (UX), User Interface (UI) design, Ergonomics and Research. After this great experience, I decided to improve my programming skills. So I became a Full-Stack developer at Sopra Steria, where I'm currently working.

What drives me in my work is to make people’s life easier. Design and create technology that feels so natural, they forget how complicated it is.
